Time to Travel

The best time to visit Portofino and its surrounding natural beauty is from May to September, when the
weather is warm and ideal for exploring. During these months, hiking trails in Portofino National Park are
fully accessible, and the vibrant harbor atmosphere is at its peak. Portofino, a charming fishing village
on the Italian Riviera, captivates visitors with its colorful buildings, scenic coastline, and clear
waters—making it a favorite for both tourists and the yachting community.
